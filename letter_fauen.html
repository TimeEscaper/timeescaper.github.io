I`m Timur, a student of Bauman Moscow State Technical University, and I am really glad to see you reading this letter! So I follow the way of «horizontal scaling» in education — I keen on all branches of science and technology and never stand on one place! And as a true engineer, I`m not afraid to expand my mind and deal with things which I face for the first time!

At the moment, my top skills relates to Programming, Mathematics (it is important that I`m an adherent of Pure Mathematics), Electronics and Physics. And, of course things like Chemistry, Biology and Medicine are the things that cannot be bypassed by my way to becoming a progressive guy! In fact, I am not simply interested in things, related to Biology, like Bioinformatics, Biotechnology and Neural Science — I`m inspired by them! I find that they are areas that unite all most perspective branches of science, including booming Machine Learning and Artifitial Intelligence. So, the report about drug design at one Data science conference showed me the true beauty of science in using Biology, Informatics and even Physics at one process that helps to save human human lifes! Another fact that I guess will be interesting for you. Today in Russia two most fashionable things — they are Machine Learning and Biotechnology. Maybe, more fashinable then they is only Philosophy, especially classical German philosohy. It sounds weird, but it is actually true!

Yes, about Philosophy and other humanitarian science. You can find it a bit strange, but I find them as useful as other, and when my friends suffered at History lessons, I just enjoyed talking to teacher and think in another, not mathematical way — it really helps to expand mind! To sum up, I can`t not to dive into German atmoshpere where masterpieces of Math (yes, I agree with my teacher that Carl Gauss was maybe the greatest mathematician ever), Physics (Heisenberg`s Uncertainty principle and Schrödinger's cat are the kind of «scientific sugar» :) ), Philosophy (now you know it`s very popular in Russia!), and, of course, Culture (Richard Wagner and German power metal and dark folk bands are enough to show its power!) where born.
